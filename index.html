Workshop Methodology

Concept to output in every session

1. Concept Introduction

Real problem framing: exams, research, productivity, career, branding, or projects.

2. Tool Demonstration

3. Guided Workflow

Step-by-step progression from raw inputs to practical results and review checkpoints.

4. Practical Output

Each day ends with a student-owned deliverable and next-step implementation action.
