# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Students, college applicants, university learners, early professionals, creators, founders, and ambitious beginners who want to use AI for real academic, career, content, business, and project outcomes.

## Product Purpose

Lumenet Labs presents a free live workshop that helps learners move beyond random AI answers into structured workflows, personal systems, and usable outputs.

Success means a visitor quickly understands the value, trusts the brand, and registers through the Google Form with minimal friction.

## Brand Personality

Calm, intelligent, premium, precise, and future-facing. The brand should feel like a thoughtful lab, not a loud AI startup.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic AI-purple SaaS templates, overloaded neon graphics, heavy video backgrounds, gimmicky 3D, cluttered card grids, and mobile layouts that break inside Instagram's in-app browser.

## Design Principles

- Lead with one memorable idea: Beyond Answers.
- Use restraint as a premium signal.
- Make the path from prompts to systems visually clear.
- Prioritize mobile readability and fast loading over decorative effects.
- Keep every CTA direct, honest, and low-friction.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

The site should stay readable on low-end phones and in-app browsers, respect reduced-motion preferences, maintain clear focus states, preserve semantic HTML, and avoid relying on animation alone to communicate meaning.
